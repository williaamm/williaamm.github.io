How to use this program

key

your key is the current data that you have associated with your account. it contains any settings you have set, and any task information. in the program, you can import your key by pasting it into the appropriate text box, loading all information provided by the key into the program. in the event of you leaving the program, you can export your current key, which can be saved separately and pasted in the next time you enter the program.
